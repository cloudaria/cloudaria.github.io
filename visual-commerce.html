Social Commerce

Your customers are your best salespeople – proudly sharing their purchases every day. Source the relevant images and videos from social networks or upload directly, curate them and publish as widgets integrated in your product pages or collections pages. Each user image is tagged with the relevant product(s), which can be shopped right from within the widget.

 

Robust tools behind-the-scenes power Cloudaria Visual Commerce, including customizable media management solutions and real-time analytics. It seamlessly integrates with your commerce platform – paste a few lines of code and you’re done! Match customer photos to your products, save time and keep fresh, authentic visual media on your online storefront.

Customer Stories

Social proof in the form of customer stories with pictures and videos impact buying behavior more than any 1:many marketing initiative. Easily curate customer photos with content, and showcase them on your web and mobile properties.

 

You can integrate customer stories as a plugin on your homepage or category pages or even as a separate page with a full feed embed. Once you set it up, content is aggregated automatically and published seamlessly across all your channels.
